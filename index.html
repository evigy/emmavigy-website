Welcome

Hi! I’m Emma Vigy, and I’m so glad you’re here. This site is my little corner of the internet — a space where I bring together all the different pieces of who I am. I’m a computer science graduate with a passion for technology, an eye for design, and a love for everything from virtual reality to equestrian sports. Whether I’m coding a website, exploring the latest in machine learning, or chasing down a polo ball on horseback, I’m driven by curiosity and a desire to keep learning, creating, and building.

This website is both a personal portfolio and a reflection of my journey. You’ll find my biography, where I share how a childhood in France led me to Wisconsin and ultimately to a degree in computer science. There’s also a detailed look at my academic experience at UW–Madison — from data structures and algorithms to virtual reality and AI. Beyond school, you’ll find the hobbies that keep me grounded: horses, fitness, Formula One, digital design, and more. Each section tells a piece of my story and the values I carry with me: passion, creativity, and drive.

Whether you’re here to learn more about my work, browse a shared interest, or just explore, I hope something here resonates with you. I’m constantly evolving — as a developer, designer, and human — and I hope this space reflects that. Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you enjoy exploring as much as I’ve enjoyed building it.
